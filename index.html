What is this?

This dashboard tracks whether the labor market currently favors job seekers or employers. It synthesizes eight key economic indicators into a single composite score to help you decide: is now a good time to look for a new job?

The score ranges from 0–100. Above 55 suggests conditions favor making a move. Below 45 suggests holding steady. The current reading reflects data from the Bureau of Labor Statistics and Federal Reserve, updated monthly.

How to read this

  • The score — A quick read on overall conditions. Higher = better for job seekers.
  • Tier 1 metrics — Direct measures of employee leverage (quit rate, hiring velocity).
  • Tier 2 metrics — Broader economic context (unemployment, Fed policy).
  • Path to Lean Favorable — What would need to change for conditions to improve.
